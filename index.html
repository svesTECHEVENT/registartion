About Events 2k23

BUG-HUNTER

This is a coding event where participants should have basic knowledge of programming languages. The participants have to solve the errors in the given programs.

INGENUITY

Join us for a day of innovation, where you will present the groundbreaking ideas. Explore the future of technology, sustainability, and creativity. Participants should identity the problem to the given themes and provide engineering solution for the identified problem.

ProjectExpo

• Project Expo is a technical event in which the participants should prepare their own project and they have to bring the project to demonstrate before the judges. There is only one round and the best is chosen by the judges and the respective prizes are given away.

TECH-INTELLECT

Join us for an exciting technical quiz where you can test your knowledge and problem-solving skills. This quiz covers a wide range of technical topics,with fellow tech enthusiasts and win exciting prizes! Don't miss this chance to challenge yourself and expand your tech horizons.

Web Wizards

This is a web development event. Case studies will be presented to the participants at the venue. They should design the website according to the given requirements.

INK & IMAGINATION

The event is to grab out the skill of arts among the participants, and the themes are related to Engineer’s day and computer science &engineering.
